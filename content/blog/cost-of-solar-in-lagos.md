# How Much Does Solar Installation Cost in Lagos? (2026 Guide)

If you're tired of unreliable grid power and expensive generator fuel, solar energy is the smartest investment you can make. But the most common question we get is: **"How much will it cost?"**

The honest answer: it depends on your specific needs. But we can give you a clear picture.

## Factors That Affect Solar Installation Costs

### 1. Your Energy Consumption
The more appliances you want to power, the larger the system you need. A basic setup for lights and fans costs less than a full-home system powering ACs and refrigerators.

### 2. System Size
- **1kW – 3kW:** Ideal for small flats, powering basic lights, fans, TV
- **5kW – 10kW:** For medium homes, including refrigerator and some ACs
- **15kW – 20kW+:** Full home or office, heavy appliances, complete off-grid

### 3. Battery Type
- **Tubular batteries:** More affordable but require maintenance
- **Lithium batteries:** Higher upfront cost but last longer, charge faster, zero maintenance

### 4. Inverter Quality
Pure sine wave inverters are essential for sensitive electronics. They cost more than modified sine wave but protect your appliances.
